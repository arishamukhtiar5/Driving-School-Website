FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you need more info, just WhatsApp us!

Can I upgrade my plan after starting?
Yes! You can upgrade from Basic to Standard or Premium at any time by paying the difference. We make it easy and flexible.
Do you offer installment payments?
Yes, we offer easy 2-installment payment plans for Standard and Premium packages. Contact us for details.
Is a free trial lesson available?
We offer a discounted first lesson for new students. Contact us to book your first session and meet your instructor before committing to a full package.
What if I fail my test?
Our pass rate is 98%. However, if you don't pass, Standard and Premium package holders get additional practice sessions at a discounted rate to prepare again.
How long does each package take to complete?
Basic: 3-4 weeks. Standard: 5-7 weeks. Premium: 8-10 weeks. All timelines depend on your session frequency and availability.
Are female instructors available for all packages?
Female instructors are available with Standard and Premium packages. Please mention your preference when booking so we can assign the right instructor.
